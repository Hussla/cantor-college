Latest News

Software Engineering Student Awards

Cantor College recently hosted the Software Engineering student of the year awards. Short-listed candidates from around the country attended the one-day event, showcasing exceptional talent and innovation in the field. The ceremony highlighted the outstanding achievements of our students and reinforced our commitment to excellence in computing education.

Staff Services

Academic Registry

For academic regulations, assessment, awards, student records, and course validation. Our registry team ensures all academic processes run smoothly and efficiently.

Catering Services

Contact the Catering Services regarding on and off-site catering for events, meetings, and special occasions. Professional catering solutions for all your needs.

Financial Services

The financial team based on the 2nd Floor is responsible for all areas of student finance as well as College budgeting, ensuring fiscal responsibility and transparency.

Information Systems Services

The ISS team delivers the College's computing facilities including all hardware and software. They also run the staff helpdesk for technical support.

Marketing Services

The Marketing Team will help promote events and new courses. They will help with press release preparation and brand management initiatives.

Personnel Services

All staff pay and conditions enquiries should be directed to the Personnel Services team on the 3rd floor. HR support for all employment matters.

Facilities Management

The facilities team is responsible for the general care and maintenance of the College. All enquiries via the main helpdesk for prompt assistance.

Professional Development

Our goal is to ensure our faculty and staff remain at the forefront of their respective fields.

  • Conference and professional event support
  • Research and sabbatical opportunities
  • Continuing education funding
  • Mentorship programs
